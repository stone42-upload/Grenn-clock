Current Challenges

The main obstacles to the circular transition.

Critical

E-waste Crisis

Exponential growth in electronic waste due to planned obsolescence. Less than 20% is properly recycled globally.

Critical

Fast Fashion

The fashion industry produces 10% of global carbon emissions. Most clothing ends up incinerated or in massive open landfills.

Major

Single-use Plastics

Plastic production continues to rise. Mechanical recycling has reached its physical and economic limits with multi-layer plastics.
